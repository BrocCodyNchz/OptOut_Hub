# OptOut Hub

One of the most comprehensive open-source data broker opt-out resources. A free web app that helps you find and remove your personal information from 80+ data brokers—people search sites, marketing brokers, credit bureaus, facial recognition, property databases, and more.

## Why OptOut Hub?

- **Most complete open-source list** — Aggregates opt-out links from BADBOOL, Optery, Privacy Rights Clearinghouse, State of Surveillance, and the California CPPA
- **California DROP Portal** — Featured entry for CA residents to submit one request to 500+ registered brokers (effective Aug 2026)
- **No data collection** — Fully client-side; your progress is stored locally in your browser only
- **Always free and open source**

## Features

- **80+ Data Brokers** — People search, marketing, credit, facial recognition, property, genealogy, business, search engines, direct mail
- **Request Removal** — One-click opens each broker's opt-out page in a new tab
- **Progress Tracking** — Mark brokers as Submitted or Removed (saved in your browser)
- **Search & Filter** — Find brokers by name, category, or status
- **Priority Ordering** — Crucial and high-priority brokers shown first
- **Sources** — Attribution modal credits all data sources

## Data Sources

- [Optery Data Brokers Directory](https://github.com/optery/optery-data-brokers-directory)
- [Privacy Rights Clearinghouse](https://privacyrights.org/data-brokers)
- [State of Surveillance](https://stateofsurveillance.org/guides/advanced/people-search-master-list/)
- [California CPPA Data Broker Registry](https://cppa.ca.gov/data_broker_registry/)

## Privacy

This app does not collect or store your personal information. All tracking is stored locally in your browser. Opt-out links open on the brokers' own sites—we do not submit requests on your behalf.
